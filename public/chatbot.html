Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is UPI?
UPI is a digital payment system that allows instant bank-to-bank transfers using a mobile phone.
2. What is the daily UPI limit?
The UPI limit is generally ₹1,00,000 per day depending on your bank.
3. How do I generate a QR code?
Open your UPI app, go to the Receive Money section and download your personal QR code.
4. Is UPI safe?
Yes, UPI is safe if you never share your PIN or OTP with anyone.
5. What to do if transaction fails?
Most failed transactions are refunded within 5-7 working days.
6. Can I use multiple UPI apps?
Yes, you can use multiple UPI apps linked to the same bank account.
7. What is UPI ID?
UPI ID is your unique payment address like mobilenumber@bank.
8. How to block UPI?
You can block UPI by contacting your bank or disabling it in app settings.
9. Are there transaction charges?
UPI transactions are generally free for customers.
10. What to do if scammed?
Contact your bank immediately and report fraud at cybercrime.gov.in.
