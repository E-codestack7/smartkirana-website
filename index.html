Introduction

Smart Kirana is a community awareness initiative focused on helping small Kirana store owners understand and adopt digital payment methods. As digital transactions grow rapidly in India, it is important for local businesses to stay updated, confident, and secure.

Purpose

To build awareness and confidence among Kirana store owners so they can safely use digital payment systems and improve daily business operations.

Project Objectives

Awareness & Education

Educate Kirana owners about UPI, QR codes, wallets, and POS systems.

Digital Safety

Promote safe practices to prevent fraud and secure transactions.

Business Growth

Show how digital payments improve efficiency and customer convenience.

Community Support

Encourage shop owners to share experiences and learn together.

Scope of Awareness

  • Focus on urban and semi-urban Kirana stores
  • Awareness of UPI, QR payments, wallets, and POS machines
  • Basic understanding of secure digital transactions
  • Guidance to help customers use digital payments confidently
